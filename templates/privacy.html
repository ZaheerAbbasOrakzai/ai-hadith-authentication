Enterprise-Grade Security: We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Your information is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers.

  • Multi-factor authentication support
  • Automated threat detection and prevention

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing hadith authentication and research services

Your Privacy Matters: We never sell your personal data. We don't share information with third parties for marketing purposes. We don't track you across the internet.

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session Cookies: To keep you logged in securely

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:
